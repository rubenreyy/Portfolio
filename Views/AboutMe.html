About Me!

Growing up in the Rio Grande Valley, I am currently a student at Texas A&M University-San Antonio. I am a computer science student exploring different computing interests to find what field I like most. As a matter of fact, I built this website from the ground up. Aside from my CS, I stay quite active within the university, serving as an Ambassador, which has provided me with innumerable opportunities. For fun, I've upkept a musical hobby for years. I play viola, as well as violin for a mariachi. I am incredibly proud to perform and show off my heritage, along with representing our university through music!
